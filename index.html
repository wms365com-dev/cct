FAQ

Common Questions

How do I start practicing?

Open Home and choose a short set, a chapter review, or Continue Where I Left Off.

Can the app read the questions out loud?

Yes. Use the mini player in Practice to read the question, pause, replay, slow the voice, and repeat a choice.

Can I change the voice and text size?

Yes. Open the Settings gear to choose English and Spanish voices, change text size, and turn on Easy View.

What does Spanish Help do?

Spanish Help translates the card and can read it in Spanish, but the main practice flow stays in English so learners stay close to the real test wording.

Will my progress stay separate from other people?

Yes. Each signed-in learner keeps separate saved cards, scores, missed questions, voices, and comfort settings.

Does this use the official study guide?

Yes. The question bank is based on the official Discover Canada guide linked above.

Is this the real citizenship test?

No. It is a practice tool designed to help learners get ready for the real test.

What if audio does not work on my phone?

Try Chrome or Edge, tap the page once first, then use Read Question again. Some mobile browsers block speech until the user interacts with the page.
